About AI Saturday Kaduna

Empowering the next generation through hands-on AI education and real-world collaboration.

Our Mission

AI Saturday Kaduna is part of a global movement to bring Artificial Intelligence learning to local communities. We believe in practical education, collaborative projects, and creating real impact. From Python to machine learning, our classes are designed to equip you with future-ready skills.

"Technology is the most powerful tool to transform society. We're building the future one Saturday at a time."

Abdurrashid Sani, Lead Instructor

Our Vision

We envision a future where young minds across Northern Nigeria harness the power of Artificial Intelligence. Our goal is to bridge the tech gap by creating a vibrant, skilled, and self-driven tech community capable of building innovative solutions to local and global challenges.
